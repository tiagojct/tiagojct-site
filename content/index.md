**Assistant Professor. Digital health, health informatics, AI in healthcare.**

I teach at the [Faculty of Medicine of the University of Porto](https://www.med.up.pt/) and research at [RISE-Health](https://www.rise-health.pt/), where digital tools meet clinical care.

My work is about translation: decision support that holds up outside slide decks, AI evaluation that survives external validation, and courses that leave health professionals genuinely fluent in their tools. Associate Editor at [*RISE Health Research Journal*](https://journals.lww.com/rise/pages/default.aspx) and [*Scientific Reports*](https://www.nature.com/srep/).

Northern Portugal. Wife, two kids, two acceptable cats.

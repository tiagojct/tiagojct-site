## Current position

**Assistant Professor** at the Department of Community Medicine, Information and Health Decision (MEDCIDS), [Faculty of Medicine of the University of Porto (FMUP)](https://www.med.up.pt/), since 22 December 2025 (tenure track, FCT-Tenure programme). Director of the [Master's in Health Informatics](https://sigarra.up.pt/fmup/en/cur_geral.cur_view?pv_curso_id=1118) (since 2 May 2026). Integrated researcher at [RISE-Health](https://cintesis.eu/pt/tiago-jacinto) (PaCeIT group, LT3: Data Science, Decision & Information Technologies). Affiliated researcher at Uppsala University. Clinical Respiratory Physiologist at CUF Porto Hospital.

## Research focus

Respiratory function assessment, AI-enabled clinical decision support, digital therapeutics and mHealth for chronic respiratory disease, and secondary analysis of large clinical datasets. Long-standing collaboration with colleagues at Uppsala University on exhaled-biomarker and type-2 inflammation research. H-index 18, 110+ publications.

## Selected projects

- [DiagCalc](/projects/diagcalc/): open diagnostic-test calculator used in teaching.
- FeNO Interpretation Aid: clinical decision support for exhaled nitric oxide interpretation.
- AIRDOC: mHealth platform for self-management of chronic obstructive respiratory disease.
- InspirerMundi: inhaler-adherence app with image-based verification.
- iNTERVENE: EU4Health-funded project (2025–2028) developing a digital framework for person-centred palliative and end-of-life care across a five-country consortium.

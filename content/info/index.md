## English {#english}

### Small {#english-small}

Tiago Jacinto is an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Porto (FMUP) and an Integrated Researcher at RISE-Health, working in digital health, medical informatics, and artificial intelligence in healthcare.

### Medium {#english-medium}

Tiago Jacinto is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Community Medicine, Information and Health Decision Sciences (MEDCIDS), Faculty of Medicine of the University of Porto (FMUP), where he carries out teaching and research in digital health, medical informatics, and artificial intelligence in healthcare. He is an Integrated Researcher at RISE-Health and an Affiliated Researcher at the Department of Women's and Children's Health at Uppsala University, Sweden. His research focuses on the development and evaluation of clinical decision support systems, mobile health applications, and the application of artificial intelligence to health data and health systems. He has participated in multiple nationally and internationally funded projects. He is Associate Editor of *RISE Health Research Journal* and *Nature Scientific Reports* and the author of more than 110 indexed scientific publications.

### Full {#english-full}

Tiago Jacinto is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Community Medicine, Information and Health Decision Sciences (MEDCIDS), Faculty of Medicine of the University of Porto (FMUP), where he carries out teaching and research in digital health, medical informatics, and artificial intelligence in healthcare.

He is an Integrated Researcher at RISE-Health and an Affiliated Researcher at the Department of Women's and Children's Health at Uppsala University, Sweden. His research focuses on the development and evaluation of clinical decision support systems, mobile health applications, and the application of artificial intelligence to health data and health systems. He has participated in multiple nationally and internationally funded projects. He is Associate Editor of *RISE Health Research Journal* and *Nature Scientific Reports* and the author of more than 110 indexed scientific publications.

At FMUP, he is Director of the Master's in Health Informatics and responsible for curricular units in Health Informatics Evaluation Methodology, Electronic Clinical Records, Productivity Tools, Digital Health, Scientific Communication and Publication, and Diagnostic Studies — across the Bachelor's in Digital Health and Biomedical Innovation, the Integrated Master's in Medicine, the Doctoral Programme in Clinical and Health Services Research, and the Doctoral Programme in Health Data Science.
